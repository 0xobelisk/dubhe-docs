## Movement

![movement](https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_banners/1632504432746192896/1711047198/1500x500)

The Movement Network is a Move-based L2 on Ethereum.

It comes with

- [cli](/movement/cli) The cli is used for building and developing a Movement project.
- [client](/movement/client) The client is used for building and developing a Movement project.
- [schemas](/movement/schemas) The schemas is used for building and developing a schemas.
- [tutorials](/movement/tutorials) The tutorials is used for building and developing a tutorials.
- [examples](/movement/examples) The examples is used for building and developing a examples.
