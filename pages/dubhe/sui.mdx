## Sui
![sui](https://pbs.twimg.com/profile_banners/1428872926363754497/1715388255/1500x500)

Sui, a next-generation smart contract platform with high throughput, low latency, and an asset-oriented programming model powered by the Move programming language

It comes with

- [cli](/sui/cli) The cli is used for building and developing a Sui project.
- [client](/sui/client) The client is used for building and developing a Sui project.
- [schemas](/sui/schemas) The schemas is used for building and developing a schemas.
- [tutorials](/sui/tutorials) The tutorials is used for building and developing a tutorials.
- [examples](/sui/examples) The examples is used for building and developing a examples.
