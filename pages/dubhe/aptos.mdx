## The Dubhe CLI

The Dubhe CLI is used for building and developing a Move project by Dubhe Engine.

It comes with

- [sui-cli](/dubhe/sui-cli) The sui-cli is used for building and developing a Sui project.
- [aptos-cli](/dubhe/aptos-cli) The aptos-cli is used for building and developing a Aptos project.
- [rooch-cli](/dubhe/rooch-cli) The rooch-cli is used for building and developing a Rooch project.
- [initia-cli](/dubhe/initia-cli) The initia-cli is used for building and developing a Initial project.
- [movement-cli](/dubhe/movement-cli) The movement-cli is used for building and developing a Movement project.
- [phad-cli](/dubhe/phad-cli) The phad-cli is used for building and developing a Phad project.
