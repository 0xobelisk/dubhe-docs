## Aptos

Aptos is a layer 1 blockchain built to support the widespread use of blockchain through better technology and user experience.

It comes with

- [cli](/aptos/cli) The cli is used for building and developing a Aptos project.
- [client](/aptos/client) The client is used for building and developing a Aptos project.
- [schemas](/aptos/schemas) The schemas is used for building and developing a schemas.
- [tutorials](/aptos/tutorials) The tutorials is used for building and developing a tutorials.
- [examples](/aptos/examples) The examples is used for building and developing a examples.
